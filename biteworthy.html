Biteworthy

Biteworthy - the brainchild of my good friend and colleague @shadoath - is an app aimed at removing the guesswork from restaurant menus. Biteworthy recommends retaurants and menu items to users based on personal preferences using parameters from category of food all the way down to dietary restrictions and individual ingredients.
